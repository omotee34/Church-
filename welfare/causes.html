Pastor Richard .A. Ige

About Our Pastor

Richard Oluwatoyin Ige, is a multi gifted International charismatic Preacher. He is a graduate of Federal University of Agriculture, Abeokuta and also holds a Bachelor Degree in Christian Education from Life Theological Seminary Ikorodu, Nigeria. He obtained Diploma and Advance Diploma certificate from ISOM, International School of Ministry, and also obtained his PHD from International college of Ministry an Affiliate of Ames Christian university USA. He is the President of the Covenant Lifeway Christian Ministry and Presiding Pastor Covenant Lifeway Church with headquarters in Abeokuta, Ogun State. He is also the International Director of prayercorpsonline, an army of global believers praying beyond wall. He is the Chief executive director of TMDI, The Martinson's development initiatives.He is a leader if leader and a spiritual father to many upcoming young ministers and entrepreneurs, A gifted man with a true Apostolic and Prophetic call on his life which is traceable to an heavenly encounter as a teenager, when he heard the voice from heaven saying I am sending you as prophet to the nation He has been of tremendous blessing to several thousands and Churches in Africa and Europe He is married to Rose Adunni, The Co Pastor of the Church and a great Teacher and Preacher of the word, their marriage is blessed with Princess, Emmanuel, Lawrence and Enoch-Martins
